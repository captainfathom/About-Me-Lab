Work Experience

I've worked in development for years, but I have been developing people. First as a pastor, then at a university, and finally in the non-profit world. I have learned how to engage people, how to listen, and how to help folks find their passion and impact in the world.

Goals

I will be a software developer. I want to build things. I want to make things work and find creative solutions. I came to this field after working for folks experiencing homelessness. I wished someone would create a way to give money to folks on the street electronically. I tried, but I needed a developer and no one came to my rescue. Fortunately, someone created this app, but it set me on a path to become the developer I needed. I want to make something to help folks in need. Here I go.
